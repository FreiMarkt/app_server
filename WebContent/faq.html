How to use a timebank? Follow 5 simple steps!
Become A Member

sign in
create your profile
insert your offers (services and/or goods)

Search for Services / Goods

use the searching-filter on the Home-Page
click your categories and subcategories
eventually search through keywords
get your RESULTS
connect to the person who offers (via email / phone)
set the meeting and exchange clear

Exchange Services/Goods

contact and meet the member who you want to deal with
get the Exchange done
reward the Trade according to the time invested:
1 hour = 1 BiX

Reward the Trade with BiX-Transaction

reward the Trade according to the time invested: 1 hour = 1 BiX
Goods are given a value in BiX by the person who offers them on T-BiX
Transact the BiX from one users BiX-account to the other users BiX account via SMS!
I want to give you 3 BiX, because you where doing me a service for 3 hours, so I send SMS to the Tel Nr of T-BiX : 333 331331
on the SMS I write:
• User-Code of the Person I want to give my BiX
p.ex.: A4B7 • then hash-sign #
• then the amount of BiX 3
• then hash sign #
• then my own User-Code p.ex: B8R6

the SMS will go to T-BiX, take 3 BiX from my personal account, put them on the other Users account, and the other user will get a confirmation SMS to show the 3 BiX have been accredited.
